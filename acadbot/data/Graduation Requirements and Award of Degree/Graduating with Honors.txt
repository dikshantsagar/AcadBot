A  student  enrolled  in  a  B.Tech.program  may also  graduate  with  Honors,  provided  the student   completes   all   the   additional   requirements   for   Honors,   as   specified   by   the 
17regulations  for  the  program  in  which  he/she  is  enrolled.  These  additional  requirements normally should include:a)The student earns at least 12 extra discipline credits.b)The student’s program includes a B.Tech. project.c)The student has a CGPA of 8.0 or more at graduation.
