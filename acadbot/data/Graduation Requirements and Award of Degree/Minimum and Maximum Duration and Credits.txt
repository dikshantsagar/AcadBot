The  minimum  duration  for  completing  the B.Tech.program  is  8  semesters(excluding  the semesters  in  which  the  student  took  semester  leave  or  the  repeated semester/year).  The minimum  number  of  credits  for  a  B.Tech.program  is  156(including  2  credits  each  of  SG and CW). The maximum duration within which the requirements must be completed is six years  (12  semesters)(including  the  semester  in  which  student  took  semester  leave  or  the repeated semester/year)
