As  mentioned  in  Ordinances,  a  letter  grade,once  awarded,shall  not  be  changed  unless the  request  is  made  by  theInstructor-in-Charge  of  the  course  and  is  approved  by  the Chairman, Senate. Any such request for change of grade must, however, be made within four weeks of the start of the next semester with all relevant records and justification
