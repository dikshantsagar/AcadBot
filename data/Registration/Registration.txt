(1)At the beginning of each semester, a student shall register for the courses he/she wishes to  take  in  that  semester.  A  student  shall  normally  be  allowed  to  register  for  a  course only if he/she has passed all the necessary pre-requisites for that course.(2)Registration is compulsory for all students who are not on leave of absence, and is the sole  responsibility  of  the  student  and  must  be  completed  before  the  last  date  of registration. Failure to register may lead to termination of the student for that semester
